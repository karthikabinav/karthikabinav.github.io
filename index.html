Karthik Abinav Sankararaman

My name is pronounced as Kar-thik(Car-thick) A-bi-nav(A as in Car-B as letter b-now) Sank-ar-a-raman(Sunk-Car-A as in Car-Ramen).

I'm a second year PhD student in the department of Computer Science at University of Maryland, College Park. I am advised by Dr. Aravind Srinivasan.

My research interests lie broadly in Design, Analysis and Applications of Algorithms. I am interested in devising algorithms which both contribute to push the state-of-art in theory as well as produce tangible impact in real-world applications.

Previously, I obtained a B.Tech(Hons), with a major in Computer Science and a minor in Operations Research, from Indian Institute of Technology, Madras. My brother and I spent our childhood days in the beautiful city of Bangalore, India.

    Adobe Inc.

    I spent the Summer of 2015 at Adobe Systems Inc, at their San Jose office. I worked with the Algorithms team inside the Digital Marketing team. I worked on designing algorithms for the Entity Resolution problem in the Sales database. These algorithms helped reduce human efforts within the sales team, drastically.

    Teritree Technologies

    I was a summer intern at this bangalore-based startup between May and July 2012. During my period there, I was involved in creating a recommendation system for user interests, based on previously collected data about him/her from social network sites and his/her activity on the system.

    I played an interesting role at this emerging Silicon Valley/Chennai based startup during May and July 2014. This company was started by a few of my batchmates from IITM.
